Timeless Contemplation

Ink & paper, 2024

‘Timeless Contemplation’ is a healing practice—a pause for stillness and an invitation to savor slowness and silence. Suspended from the ceiling, a graceful 10-foot-long white scroll gently meets a modest podium, while the remaining scroll rests in a poised roll. Inscribed with meticulous symbols in white ink, derived from the artist's ingenious system, the scroll invites viewers into a meditative sequence. This act of inscription becomes a contemplative exercise, inviting reflection and introspection. Through an ongoing, infinite counting process, the artist symbolizes a journey towards infinity, transcending the confines of time and space.
