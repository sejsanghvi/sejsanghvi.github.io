Publication Design—'the art of less'

Book—'the art of less' is a collaboration project that delves into the world of minimalism in art, design, and fashion. Drawing upon the insightful research conducted by my collaborator, 'the art of less' has been crafted as a true embodiment of the principles of minimalism—simple, elegant, and sleek.

I'm Sejal Sanghvi, a Visual Designer and Fine Artist, passionate about creating pixel-perfect designs across brand identities, apps, prototyping, coding, and product management. Whether it's through design, code, or a blend of both, I'm committed to creating user-centric digital solutions.
