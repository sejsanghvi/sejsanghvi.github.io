Shade

Projection on wall, 2023

Shade is a serene and meditative room through the interplay of changing shadows, drawing inspiration from Zen Buddhism's principles of impermanence and tranquillity. It emphasizes minimal aesthetics, prioritizing the visitor's inner experience over physical objects, and incorporates binaural theta beats to reduce anxiety and induce relaxation. Natural light is captured, processed, and reintroduced into the space, fostering a space away from the noise; a space to pause; a space to reconnect. This body of art is a space to breathe; to witness time; to acknowledge impermanence.
