Background

GEOH empowers home health care agencies with an app featuring real-time billing, intuitive design, and FSSA-compliant capabilities.

The GEOH Accounts Page is a financial management tool designed to simplify invoicing and payments. Its UX design emphasizes user-friendly simplicity to enhance efficiency, reduce paperwork, and ensure compliance. This intuitive interface provides actionable insights, streamlining processes and supporting overall agency success.

Designer's Note

This project was for GEOH, where I was tasked with rebranding and designing the Accounts Page. The creative brief emphasized enhancing usability and functionality while maintaining compliance with industry standards. By focusing on intuitive workflows and user-friendly design principles, I aimed to streamline financial management for home health care agencies. The interface prioritizes efficiency, reducing administrative burdens and fostering operational success through accessible, compliant tools.



Let's Dig Deeper into the Specifics

Problem Statement

The accounts page for GEOH requires redesigning to streamline the user flow and declutter the interface. Users need to manage invoices, make payments, view contracts, and access detailed information about agencies and clients. The challenge is to enhance usability by organizing information effectively, simplifying navigation, and ensuring a smooth user experience throughout.

Branding | GEOH

My Account

Homepage prioritizes user-friendliness and accessibility, focusing on the Invoices tab for easy viewing and managing. Adjacent tabs provide quick access to payment and contract details, ensuring effortless financial management.

Invoice Details

Clicking on an invoice directs users to the Invoice Details page, displaying comprehensive information. Here, users can review detailed invoice data and conveniently proceed to make payments. The Pay button is subtly highlighted for easy visibility.

Make Payment

On the Make Payment page, top boxes show Payment Due, Total Balance, and Selected Invoices. Users select invoices to pay or the total balance. A prominent Pay button at the bottom ensures easy access, guiding users through the payment process seamlessly.

Select Method of Payment

Clicking the Pay button directs users to the Select Method of Payment page. Here, users can choose their payment method, add or remove cards, and set a default option.

Contracts

The Contracts tab provides quick access to all contractual documents, ensuring users can easily manage and reference their agreements as needed.
